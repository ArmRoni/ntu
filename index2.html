DEFINE YOUR
CAREER HORIZONS

To identify career opportunities, understand your strengths and be open to explore new career pathways. Look beyond traditional roles in your field of study, and keep up with changing technology, business practices as well as the evolving global economy. Incorporate your values, occupational interests, personality style and skills (VIPS) into your long-term career development.

WHAT DRIVES YOU?

Before charting your career path, find out your personal values, then consider your ideal work environment and lifestyle. Align your professional goals with what you are passionate about and what excites you. Think about the types of jobs and industries you want to explore. Do some self-reflection and see your Career Coach, who can help you discover your VIPS and kick-start your career exploration.

WHAT ARE YOUR SKILLS, STRENGTHS AND TALENTS?

Conduct a personal skills audit to list your best technical and soft skills. Identify the transferable skills you have gained through studies, group work, professional experiences, and even co-curricular activities (CCAs). These could include analytical skills, persuasion and negotiation skills, and project management. Set learning goals for yourself in terms of new skills you want to gain, and the new fields you can apply your current and future skills to.

WHAT IS OUT THERE?

Research market trends and outlook, as well as the challenges in the industries of your interest. Talk to family members, friends, seniors and alumni in these industries. Find out what led them to their career choices, and how they have grown and persevered in their jobs. This will help you understand how you can contribute and make your mark at the workplace. Determine your personal and professional goals for the next five to ten years, and map how you are going to get there.

WHAT CAN YOU EXPLORE AND LEARN?

With a list of potential industries and job roles, seek to understand and immerse yourself in opportunities to learn and experience more. Do not be afraid to enter job scopes and organisations which you have never considered. Be curious and hungry to discover the full range of career opportunities available, through job shadowing, internships and career events. Speak with our Career Consultants to gain deeper insights into different industries.

Keep an open mind, stay flexible and adaptable to challenges and opportunities you encounter in your job search and career journey. Stay in touch with emerging trends to be relevant in the job market. Enjoy the ride!
